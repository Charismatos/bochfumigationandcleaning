In this Part of the World pests are more than just a nuisance; they are a threat to your health, property, and business operations. At BOCH Fumigation and Cleaning, we specialize in the rapid and guaranteed elimination of the region's most common and destructive invaders.

Top 5 Pests BOCH Eliminates

Here are the most common pests we encounter in homes and businesses, along with the specific risks they pose and our targeted solutions.

  1. Termites (Enkuyege) üêú

    The silent destroyers, often unnoticed until significant damage is done.

    Threat to You BOCH's Expert Solution
    Structural Damage: They eat through wood, weakening the structure of your home, beams, and furniture

    Pre- and Post-Construction Treatment: We create a chemical soil barrier around your foundation and use localized injection treatments to eliminate existing colonies, protecting your investment for years.
  2. Rodents (Rats & Mice) üêÄ

    Highly adaptable and notoriously difficult to control without professional help.

    Threat to You BOCH's Expert Solution
    Disease Transmission: Carry diseases like Lassa fever and contaminate food and surfaces with urine and droppings

    Secure Baiting & Exclusion: We use tamper-proof bait stations and traps, focusing on entry point sealing (exclusion) and eliminating nesting sites to prevent their return.

    Fire Hazard: They constantly chew, often severing electrical cables hidden within walls.
  3. Cockroaches (Ebiyenjje)

    Common in warm, humid areas, they quickly infest kitchens and storage spaces.

    Threat to You BOCH's Expert Solution
    Allergies & Asthma: Their droppings and shed skins trigger allergies and asthma, especially in children

    Bait Gels and Residual Spraying: We use odorless, high-performance bait gels for internal spaces and apply residual fumigants to cracks, crevices, and drainage points for long-term control.
    Food Poisoning: They carry and transfer bacteria like Salmonella and E. coli onto food preparation surfaces.
  4. Bed Bugs (Ebiku)

    One of the most persistent and hard-to-eliminate pests, notorious for causing significant distress.

    Threat to You BOCH's Expert Solution
    Painful Bites & Sleeplessness: Feed on human blood, causing itchy, irritating bites and disrupting rest

    Mattress and Upholstery Steam Treatment: We employ a two-part solution: high-heat steam cleaning to kill eggs and adult bugs instantly, followed by a targeted residual chemical spray in surrounding cracks and furniture.

    Rapid Spread: They spread easily through luggage, clothing, and second-hand furniture.
  5. Mosquitos (Ensili)

    The most dangerous public health pest in the region.

    Threat to You BOCH's Expert Solution
    Disease Vector: Primary carriers of diseases prevalent in Uganda, including Malaria and Dengue fever Targeted Fogging & Larvicide: We conduct outdoor fogging/spraying (compound, gardens, drains) to kill adult mosquitos and apply larvicides to stagnant water sources to break the breeding cycle.
    Rapid Spread:

    They spread easily through luggage, clothing, and second-hand furniture.
